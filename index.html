Introduction & Consent

Dear Expert,

Thank you for participating in this study on the use of date palm biomass as a building material in Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region. Your expert judgment will be used to determine the relative importance of three evaluation criteria using the Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP):

Technical Performance (strength, thermal, durability)  ·  Environmental Impact (embodied carbon, energy)  ·  Supply Chain (cost, local availability)

The survey has two parts. Part 1 asks you to compare the three criteria in pairs. A Consistency Ratio (CR) is calculated in real time — if your CR exceeds 0.10 you will be given one opportunity to revise. Part 2 asks about your professional perceptions of date palm in the built environment.

Your responses are confidential and used solely for academic research.
